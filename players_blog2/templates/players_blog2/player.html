Stephen Curry

Player Overview

Stephen Curry is a professional basketball player who plays for the Golden State Warriors in the NBA. Widely regarded as one of the greatest shooters in basketball history, Curry has transformed the game with his shooting range and skill.

Career Highlights

  • 3× NBA Champion (2015, 2017, 2018)
  • 2× NBA Most Valuable Player (MVP) (2015, 2016)
  • NBA All-Star (multiple selections)
  • NBA scoring champion (2016)
